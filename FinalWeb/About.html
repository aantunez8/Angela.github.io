About Us

In Bear Fighters, we seek to inform the public of the danger of bears and the secret they've been hiding, which is the ability to fight like a martial arts master. While they might seem cuddly and friendly, make no mistake, they are cruel and savage when they perform their martial arts. That being said, we'd like to raise awareness among people about how vicious bears can be when they feel threatened.
